About

CzechTutor is a free and open source web app for helping Czech / English language students.

Lessons can be configured at various lengths and difficulties. Once a lLesson is completed, it is marked and graded with feedback for the student.

Czech / English phrases and vocabulary were sourced from:

  • https://www.manythings.org/anki/
  • https://apps.ankiweb.net/
  • https://tatoeba.org/en
